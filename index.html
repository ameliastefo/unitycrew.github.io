About UnityCrew

UnityCrew is a platform designed to help tech professionals and entrepreneurs find and create ideal teams for various projects. Whether it's for a hackathon, school project, or startup, UnityCrew makes it easy to connect with the right people based on your skills and strengths. The idea for this webpage originated from a challenge presented at a JunctionX competition, where participants were tasked with creating a solution to facilitate team formation and collaboration in the tech industry.
